Outstatic is an open-source CMS for Markdown and MDX sites that stores content directly in your GitHub repository. It gives teams a clean, intuitive editing experience without sacrificing ownership, version control, or developer workflows. Use it hosted or self-hosted to manage content with the simplicity of a modern CMS and the flexibility of Git.
