A practical little developer tool for generating visual before-and-after comparisons of web changes. It lets you capture screenshots from two URLs or images, supports element-specific and full-page diffs, and can output markdown-ready comparisons for pull requests, making UI review much easier.

Built by [**James Clements**](https://x.com/jamesvclements).
